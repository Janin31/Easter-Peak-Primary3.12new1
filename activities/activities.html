What We Offer!

We offers learners the best schooling experience possible with many activities and after school curricular.

Inter House Activities

As a part of our holistic approach to education, we aim to equip and encourage our learners to be involved in various non-academic activities.

Inter House activities which are athletics, are taking place throughout the year.

Learners are placed into 4 houses and complete with each other for the best group awards presented at the end of each term.

Sports

Soccer , Netbal and Marching Band

Currently we offer a number of different sports at school namely: Soccer , Netball and Marching Parad.

Our sports are offered by professional trained athletes. Training would include learning different rules and manovoures of the games; also we have stamina training once every second week that would include doing various exercises to boost our stamina that we have an extra edge on the other teams.

Holiday Program

We offer our kids a 3 day Holiday Program at our school. We do taeching and learning ,but most importantly we have FUN, FUN and More FUN.
